What We Offer:

Creatie Property Resolutions, LLC helps homeowners eliminats their liability and worry when faced with leaving their home. CPR also (1) provides the homeowner with funds to relocate or cover the costs of a bankrupcy filing, (2) prepares all of the transfer documents, and (3) covers the costs of the homeowner's attorney at closing and all related filing fees

In return, CPR undertakes the homeownere's prior burden of protecting and preserving the property until it is sold or returned to the lender, which translates into safer neighborhoods and communities. And CPR protects thehomeowner's reputation by discreetly transferring the house to new ownership.
